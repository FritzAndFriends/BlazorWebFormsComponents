### AngleSharp Feasibility Analysis
- Evaluated AngleSharp as replacement for System.Xml.Linq.XDocument in AspxParser.cs
- Analyzed parser capabilities against Phase 1 ASPX middleware requirements
- Tested prototype for:
  - Unclosed HTML tags (`<br>`, `<hr>`, `<input>`)

- ✅ Mitigation: Keep existing `asp:` → `asp_` rename before parsing
- ✅ Guardrails: Mandatory regex pre-processing, whitespace validation, performance benchmarking

### Decision & Recommendation
